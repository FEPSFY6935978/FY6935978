Abstract

    The purpose of this paper will be to find an average time to complete the Omega Ruby/Alpha Sapphire Regional
    Pokedex, Shiny Regional Pokedex, National Pokedex,and Shiny National Pokedex. The average time will be
    calculated using a combination of personal gameplay, calculated encounter times and catch rates, and average 
    gameplay times found online. The average time I will be calculate may not be entirely accurate, and relies on
    luck with encounters and averages on a large scale, especially for the Shiny Pokedexes where trying to
    actually complete them would take an extremely long time.
